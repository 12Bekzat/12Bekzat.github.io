About company
We carefully select materials for the production of furniture, from upholstery and fittings to structural details that are insignificant at first glance.
Design
Orientation to world trends in furniture fashion and a flexible approach to the development of new models allowed us to become a trendsetter and get on the pages of specialized publications in a short period of time: AD, ELLE Decoration, Houzz, The Village. Ownership of production processes in synergy with trade knowledge gives us the opportunity to be the first to produce demanded furniture on the Kazakhstan market.
Prices
An affordable price for the product is our direct goal. Control over all stages of production and the absence of long chains of intermediaries, from the purchase of raw materials to sales methods, allow us to reduce the distance between the store and the consumer and offer high quality products that are accessible to most users.
Change is easy
The basic solution did not fit? It's easy to change! Most of the goods in the store can be customized: pick upholstery in the color of the carpet or find pet-friendly fabric, plan the filling of the wardrobe for the needs of a large family, add a bed with a lifting mechanism. Our online constructor will help you do it quickly, try it!
Innovation
We constantly improve our product and attract partners who specialize in the production of advanced materials. So, for example, we created a line of upholstered furniture with the innovative Elax filler, a super-elastic foam designed for 20 years of regular use. We also have options for upholstery from the Chistetika series - easy-to-clean "smart" fabrics.
